# ISEC - Organizational Strategy (EO) - DMDI Strategic Analysis (2025/2026)

### Context

This repository contains the project report developed for the Organizational Strategy (Estratégia Organizacional) course unit, part of the Computer Engineering degree at the Coimbra Institute of Engineering (ISEC), during the 2025/2026 academic year.

### Project Goal

The primary objective was to conduct a comprehensive strategic and internal diagnosis of DMDI - Eyewear Solutions, an optics distributor operating in Portugal and PALOP regions. The project aimed to evaluate the company's competitive positioning, identify operational inefficiencies (specifically in logistics and after-sales), and propose IT-driven business process reengineering to strengthen its market advantage.

### Key Aspects Covered

*   **External Strategic Analysis:** Application of PEST analysis, Porter's 5 Forces, BCG Matrix, Strategic Groups, and Critical Success Factors (FCS) to evaluate the macroscopic and competitive environment of the optics sector.
*   **Internal Diagnosis:** In-depth evaluation of DMDI's Value Chain and current Information Systems maturity using the McFarlan Matrix.
*   **Business Process Reengineering (BPMN):** Mapping and modeling of "As-Is" and "To-Be" workflows for critical operations: Supplier Order Management and After-Sales Service (SAV).
*   **IT Solution Proposals:** Specification of two new custom modules for the company's ERP (Artsoft) to automate delivery tracking, eliminate manual paper-based ticketing, and provide real-time traceability.
*   **Strategic Alignment:** Development of a Balanced Scorecard (BSC) to translate the proposed technological upgrades into measurable financial, customer, internal process, and learning goals.

### Tools and Frameworks Used

*   **Strategic Frameworks:** SWOT, PEST, Porter's 5 Forces, BCG Matrix, McFarlan Matrix, Balanced Scorecard (BSC)
*   **Process Modeling:** BPMN (Business Process Model and Notation)
